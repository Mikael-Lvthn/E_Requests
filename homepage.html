About Urbiztondo

Welcome to Urbiztondo, a vibrant municipality in Pangasinan, known for its strong community, agricultural roots, and steady progress. With 21 barangays, our town embraces unity, resilience, and tradition, while supporting sustainable development and public service excellence. Join us in celebrating our rich heritage and working towards a brighter future for all residents. Our Kanen Festival is a joyful celebration of culture, delicacies, and togetherness.

Experience Urbiztondo—where tradition meets progress, and everyone feels at home.
